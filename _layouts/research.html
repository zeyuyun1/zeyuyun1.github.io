Short Bio

I was born and raised in China in Jinan and Qingdao, China. I came to US alone at aged 15 and graduated at Clovis North High School in Fresno, CA. I graduated from UC Berkeley with a double major in Computer Sciecne and Mathmetics with highest distinction. During my undergradute, I'm very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Bruno Olshausen and Prof. Yubei Chen. I'm also very fortunate to be member of Redwood Center for Theoretical Neuroscience.
